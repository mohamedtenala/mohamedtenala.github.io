CALL CENTER ANALYTICS

Call Center Performance Analytics

A Power BI dashboard designed to monitor daily call center performance, agent productivity, service levels, appointment bookings and inbound and outbound call activity.

INDUSTRY Healthcare
TOOLS Power BI . SQL . DAX
FOCUS Service Level · Productivity · Staffing

BUSINESS CHALLENGE

Call center performance was difficult to evaluate from fragmented operational reports.

Management needed one centralized view of inbound and outbound activity, service levels, appointment bookings, agent performance and daily workload distribution. Existing reporting made it difficult to identify peak hours, monitor abandonment and compare agent productivity.

MY APPROACH

Built a daily operational monitoring dashboard.

I designed a Power BI dashboard that consolidated call center performance into a single interactive reporting solution for operational review and staffing decisions.

  • Monitored inbound and outbound call volumes.
  • Tracked service level and abandoned-call performance.
  • Analyzed average waiting, handling and wrap-up times.
  • Measured appointment booking and confirmation activity.
  • Compared productivity across individual agents.
  • Identified hourly workload patterns and peak call periods.
  • Analyzed call outcomes and wrap-up classifications.
  • Supported staffing and daily operational planning.

KEY METRICS

Daily visibility into service quality and productivity.

90% Service level achieved
17 Agents monitored daily
400+ More appointments booked
-3% Abandoned-call rate

INSIGHTS DELIVERED

Improved visibility into workload, staffing and service quality.

The dashboard helped operations teams understand when call volumes peaked, how efficiently agents handled calls and where service-level risks required intervention.

  • Identified peak hours requiring higher staffing coverage.
  • Highlighted agents with high and low productivity.
  • Improved monitoring of abandoned calls and callbacks.
  • Strengthened visibility into booking conversion activity.
  • Supported daily team performance reviews.
