OPERATIONS ANALYTICS

Hospital Operations Dashboard

A centralized Power BI reporting solution designed to help leadership and operational teams monitor hospital performance, reduce manual reporting and improve decision-making.

Industry Healthcare
TOOLS Power BI · Excel · SQL . DAX
FOCUS Operations · KPIs · Reporting

BUSINESS CHALLENGE

Fragmented reporting made performance difficult to track.

Hospital performance information was distributed across multiple spreadsheets and manual reports. This made it difficult for leadership teams to access consistent, timely and reliable operational insights.

MY APPROACH

Designed a centralized performance reporting solution.

I developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that brought together operational KPIs from multiple departments into one reporting platform.

  • Defined and standardized more than 40 operational KPIs.
  • Consolidated data from different operational sources.
  • Created executive summaries and department-level views.
  • Added filters for date, department, service and location.
  • Improved data visibility for leadership and operational teams.

BUSINESS IMPACT

Reduced manual reporting and improved access to insights.

40+ KPIs monitored
28 Business Users (Row-Level Security)
12 hrs Weekly reporting Time Saved
30 m+ Records analyzed

KEY LEARNINGS

Clear KPI definitions are as important as the dashboard itself.

The project reinforced the importance of aligning KPI definitions across departments before building reports. Strong stakeholder engagement and validation were essential to ensure the dashboard was trusted and adopted.
