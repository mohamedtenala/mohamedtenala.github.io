PHYSICIAN ANALYTICS

Physician Performance Analytics

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to evaluate physician productivity, operational performance, capacity utilization and revenue contribution through a single standardized reporting view.

INDUSTRY Healthcare
TOOLS Power BI · Excel · SQL . DAX
FOCUS Physician Performance · Productivity Monitoring

BUSINESS CHALLENGE

Physician performance data was fragmented across multiple reports.

Leadership needed a consistent way to evaluate physician performance using operational, utilization and revenue indicators. Existing information was distributed across separate reports, making comparison and monthly review time-consuming.

MY APPROACH

Built a standardized physician performance framework.

I designed a Power BI dashboard that combined productivity, capacity, clinical activity and revenue indicators into one interactive physician-level view.

  • Defined standardized physician performance KPIs.
  • Added physician, specialty and month-level filtering.
  • Monitored volume and capacity utilization trends.
  • Tracked follow-up and referral performance against targets.
  • Analyzed inpatient activity and monthly trends.
  • Integrated outpatient and inpatient ARPP performance.
  • Compared actual results with budgeted performance.
  • Visualized revenue mix by service category.

KEY METRICS

A complete view of physician performance.

10+ Performance indicators monitored
Monthly Executive performance reviews supported
3 Core performance dimensions
1 Centralized physician reporting view

INSIGHTS DELIVERED

Enabled evidence-based workforce and performance decisions.

The dashboard allowed leadership to identify utilization gaps, evaluate physician workload, compare performance against targets and better understand the relationship between activity and revenue contribution.

  • Identified underutilized physician capacity.
  • Highlighted gaps in follow-up and referral performance.
  • Supported specialty and physician-level comparisons.
  • Improved visibility into outpatient and inpatient ARPP.
  • Strengthened monthly leadership performance reviews.
